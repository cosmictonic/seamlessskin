About Seamless Skin, MD

Deborah Woods, MD, was born and raised in Albuquerque, New Mexico. She attended Sandia Preparatory School for both middle school and high school, getting a master's degree from UNM in Pharmacy (PharmD), then attending medical school at UNM, where she also completed her residency in anesthesiology. At Seamless Skin, MD, which Deb founded in 2017, her goal is to use Botox and fillers to enhance natural beauty, making people feel more attractive and allowing deeper participation in taking care of themselves as they age. Deb enjoys her family, friends and being outside skiing and biking in the Land of Enchantment.
